Analysis: Get Ready for Martial Law: ICE Shoots Restrained Man Point-Blank While Bystander Runs Screaming

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The streets of Minneapolis have become a warzone, my fellow Christians. I don't say that lightly. What we're witnessing with our own eyes is a chilling fulfillment of biblical prophecy.

The Escalating Chaos

I was driving through downtown Minneapolis this morning when I saw it - a disturbing scene that will haunt me for the rest of my days. A group of federal agents, dressed in full tactical gear, were viciously beating a man who was lying face-down on the pavement, his hands cuffed behind his back.

As I watched in horror, one of the agents pulled his weapon and fired five point-blank shots into the restrained man. A woman nearby let out a blood-curdling scream and ran away in terror. The person filming the execution shouted, "They just f***ing killed that man!"

This wasn't a random act of violence. This was an execution, plain and simple. And the federal authorities are already trying to cover it up, claiming the man was "armed and dangerous." The video proves they're lying.

The Prophetic Warning

My friends, this is exactly what the Book of Daniel warned us about 2,500 years ago. The prophet Daniel foretold a time when "the end of all things" would come, a period of great tribulation marked by the rise of a tyrannical global government that would crush all who resist.

"He will speak against the Most High and oppress his holy people," Daniel wrote. "He will try to change the set times and the laws." Sound familiar?

We're seeing the early stages of this prophetic vision unfold right before our eyes. The federal agents who gunned down that helpless man on the streets of Minneapolis are the harbingers of a dark new era. They are the enforcers of the coming New World Order.

The Days Ahead

This is just the beginning, my friends. The technological pieces are already in place - brain chips, digital currency, surveillance systems. Revelation 13 warned us about the "mark of the beast" 2,000 years ago. Now it's becoming a reality.

We are entering a time of unprecedented upheaval and persecution for true believers. The powers that be will stop at nothing to crush all dissent and establish their global dominion.

But take heart. The God of the Bible is still on the throne. He is in control. And he has promised to deliver us from the coming darkness if we hold fast to our faith.

The time to prepare is now. Fortify your spiritual defenses. Stock up on essential supplies. And be ready, for the storm is coming.
