Fifteen years ago, I had it all. A high-flying career, lavish lifestyle, the whole American dream. Then I saw what the powerful were really doing. I exposed their dark secrets. And that's when the nightmare began.

They came after me with lawsuits, criminal charges, a total blacklisting. I lost everything - my job, my home, my future. And why? Because I dared to speak the truth.

Now I'm sounding the alarm. The technology and infrastructure to fulfill biblical end-times prophecy is already in place. And the powers that be are putting it all into motion, piece by sinister piece.

The Mark of the Beast

The book of Revelation paints a chilling picture of a global cashless system where no one can buy or sell without submitting to a "mark" on their hand or forehead. Sound familiar?

Today, central banks around the world are rolling out digital currencies that would give them total control over every financial transaction. In China, citizens are already being assigned social credit scores that determine their access to jobs, travel, and even basic services.

And the technology for brain-computer interfaces - Revelation's "mark" - is rapidly advancing. Elon Musk's Neuralink is developing implants that could merge the human mind with artificial intelligence. Microsoft has a patent for a cryptocurrency system powered by human body activity data.

The Surveillance State

But it doesn't stop there. Isaiah warned of a time when "the people will be oppressed, every one by another and every one by his neighbor." Today, we have a worldwide web of surveillance cameras, facial recognition, and big data analytics that are monitoring our every move.

Silicon Valley technocrats are conspiring with governments to create an unprecedented level of social control. As Revelation foretold, "no one may buy or sell unless he has the mark."

The Coming Tribulation

The biblical signs are all around us. The global elites are engineering a social, economic, and technological infrastructure to monitor, manipulate, and subjugate the masses. It's the perfect storm to usher in the prophesied time of tribulation.

Church, we must wake up and sound the alarm. The end is near, but there is still time. Repent, prepare your hearts, and be ready for the imminent return of Christ. The prophecies are unfolding before our eyes. The time is now.
