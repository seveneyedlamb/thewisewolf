Revelation 13 describes a "beast" that "forced all people, great and small, rich and poor, free and slave, to receive a mark on their right hands or on their foreheads, so that they could not buy or sell unless they had the mark." Does that sound like the cashless society and brain chips we're hurtling towards?

Epstein's Ties to Israel's Mossad

What's even more disturbing is the credible evidence that Epstein was an Israeli spy, funneling intelligence to Mossad. He had deep connections to the highest levels of the Israeli government, including his buddy Ehud Barak.

Epstein's surveillance company Carbyne was practically an extension of Mossad, staffed by former Israeli military intelligence officers. They've got a direct pipeline into American 911 calls and smartphone data. If that doesn't send a chill down your spine, I don't know what will.

The Prophetic Implications

Look, I don't know about you, but this whole situation has me seriously spooked. We're talking about convicted sex criminals, Israeli spies, global surveillance, and a chilling fulfillment of biblical end-times prophecy.

If Epstein really was an asset of Mossad, funneling intelligence and technology to Israel, then we might be staring down the barrel of the very "beast system" the Bible warned us about. A global, authoritarian regime monitoring and controlling every aspect of our lives.
