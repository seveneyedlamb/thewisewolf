Epstein Unredacted Files Expose Shocking Biblical Prophecies

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

Remember Pizzagate? That so-called "conspiracy theory" about a D.C. pizza parlor running a child trafficking ring? Well, the real pedophile network wasn't hiding in some imaginary basement. It was cruising on yachts. Partying at Mar-a-Lago. Flying on private jets. And the man currently occupying the Oval Office has his name appearing nearly one million times in the unredacted Epstein files.

One. Million. Times.

Not a typo. Not hyperbole. When I started writing this, the count was over 500,000. Hours later? Damn near doubled. I'm supposed to sit here like a good little Christian and pretend this is normal? That this is just partisan noise? Get the hell out of here with that.

The Epstein Scandal: A Fulfillment of End-Times Prophecy?

The Bible warned us about this. Decades ago, the prophet Daniel foretold a time of great wickedness and deception in the end times. He spoke of a "little horn" that would rise up, one "whose look was more stout than his fellows" (Daniel 7:20). This despicable figure would "speak great words against the most High" and "wear out the saints of the most High" (Daniel 7:25).

Does the Epstein scandal fit this disturbing prophecy? The evidence is staggering. A global elite pedophile ring operating in plain sight, with the most powerful man in the world at its center. Revelations of unspeakable evil. Attempts to silence and discredit whistleblowers. It's the stuff of biblical nightmares.

The Warning of Isaiah

The prophet Isaiah also forewarned of this hour, declaring: "Woe to those who call evil good and good evil, who put darkness for light and light for darkness" (Isaiah 5:20). Could there be a more apt description of the topsy-turvy world we inhabit, where the most depraved acts are covered up and normalized?

When the unredacted Epstein files surfaced, the cognitive dissonance was deafening. Revelations that shake the very foundations of our society, and yet so many still refuse to see. Like the proverbial frog in boiling water, we've grown numb to the evil that surrounds us.

But the time for complacency is over. The prophetic warning bells are ringing. The Epstein scandal may well be a harbinger of the tribulation to come. Are you prepared to stand firm in your faith? The hour is late, my fellow Christians. The darkness is closing in.

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
