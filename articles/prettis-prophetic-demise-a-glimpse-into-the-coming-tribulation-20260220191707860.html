The Death of a Modern-Day Martyr On a fateful Saturday morning in Minneapolis, a tragic scene unfolded that sends shockwaves through the Christian community. Federal agents pepper-sprayed a woman, prompting 37-year-old ICU nurse Alex Pretti to step in and assist her. What happened next is the stuff of nightmares - Pretti was surrounded by multiple officers, beaten while on the ground, and then shot multiple times, dying later at the hospital.

Disturbingly, the official story from the Department of Homeland Security paints Pretti as a violent threat who "approached officers menacingly with a semi-automatic handgun and dozens of rounds of ammunition." But as any critical thinker can see, that single sentence reeks of blatant lies. How exactly does one "see" dozens of rounds of ammunition? It's clear the government is covering up the cold-blooded murder of an innocent man.
