The Betrayal of the Watchmen The Bible warns that in the last days, even the spiritual leaders and "watchmen" tasked with guarding the flock will fall prey to deception and moral compromise. Ezekiel 33 describes watchmen who fail to sound the alarm, leaving the people vulnerable to destruction.

In similar fashion, the Epstein case has exposed how many prominent pastors, ministries and Christian influencers have been compromised by the lure of power, wealth and status. They have traded their prophetic voice for a place at the table of the global elite, contributing to the religious apostasy that Scripture foretells.

The Unraveling of the Ruling Class But Epstein's sordid network of abuse is just the tip of the iceberg. The same spirit of lawlessness and moral depravity has infected the highest levels of government, finance, media and entertainment. This ruling class elite is now desperately trying to maintain its grip on power as their dark secrets are laid bare.

As the walls close in, we're seeing increased censorship, civil unrest and a brazen power grab under the guise of a "Great Reset." The globalist agenda to centralize control and usher in a new world order is accelerating, just as the Bible warned.

A Time of Testing and Refinement For the faithful, these developments should come as no surprise. Jesus Himself said that in the end times, "many will fall away and will betray one another and hate one another" (Matthew 24:10). This is a season of intense spiritual warfare and a great "shaking" that will separate the wheat from the chaff.

The good news is that those who remain steadfast in their faith will emerge from this trial strengthened and purified. As the world descends into darkness, the light of God's people will shine ever brighter - a beacon of hope in a world that is quickly running out of time.
