The End is Near: How the Elite are Pushing Us Towards Biblical Prophecy

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The End is Near. That's the message I'm getting from the smoke signals of the global elite. They're making their move, and it ain't good news for the rest of us.

Look, I used to be one of them. A card-carrying member of the inner circle. Privy to their secret plans. But I couldn't stomach it anymore. The deception, the greed, the blatant disregard for human life. It was time to blow the lid off this thing.

The Great Reset You've probably heard rumblings about "The Great Reset" - the World Economic Forum's plan to radically restructure the global economy and social order. Well, let me tell you, it's no conspiracy theory. It's real, it's happening, and the end game is straight out of the Book of Revelation.

These globalist puppetmasters are methodically engineering a worldwide economic collapse. Why? To pave the way for their new digital currency and social credit system - the perfect infrastructure for the prophesied "mark of the beast."

Tribulation Timing But that's just the start. Once the global economy is in shambles, they'll unleash the next phase of their diabolical plan - manufactured food shortages, pandemics, and war. Sound familiar? It's the biblical tribulation, folks.

The signs are all there. Inflation skyrocketing. Supply chains in chaos. Conflict brewing between nations. The stage is set for the prophesied "day of the Lord" - a time of unparalleled suffering that precedes the return of Christ.

Remnant Rising Now, I know what you're thinking - "This guy's gone off the deep end!" But hear me out. I'm not some doomsday preacher ranting about the rapture. I'm an investigative journalist who's been inside the belly of the beast. And I'm telling you, the elite are making their final move.

The good news is, God always has a plan. While the wicked are preparing for their own destruction, the faithful remnant is rising up. The stage is set for the greatest spiritual awakening the world has ever seen. So don't lose hope, my friends. The battle may be intense, but the victory belongs to the Lord.
