Pretti's Execution and the Ominous Signs of the End Times

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The shocking execution-style killing of Alex Pretti by federal agents in Minneapolis this weekend has sent shockwaves through the nation. The 37-year-old ICU nurse, who was simply trying to help a woman in distress, was brutally beaten and shot multiple times by Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) officers.

The "official story" spun by Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem is a blatant lie. She claimed Pretti "approached officers menacingly with a semi-automatic handgun with dozens of rounds of ammunition." But as the original Wise Wolf report points out, that's simply not possible. Ammunition is contained in a magazine inside the gun - it's not sitting in his hands for all to see.

"For we do not wrestle against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the authorities, against the cosmic powers over this present darkness, against the spiritual forces of evil in the heavenly places" (Ephesians 6:12).

The senseless killing of Pretti, combined with the blatant lies and cover-up, has all the earmarks of demonic influence. The powers of darkness seem to be rapidly gaining ground, as godless forces tighten their grip on society.

A Season of Divine Judgment Most ominously, the Scriptures warn that in the last days, God will unleash His righteous judgment upon a wicked and unrepentant world.
