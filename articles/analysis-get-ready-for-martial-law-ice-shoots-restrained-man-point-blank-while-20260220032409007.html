The Daniel Prophecy The prophet Daniel forewarned of this very scenario over 2,500 years ago. In Daniel 7, he describes a vision of four beasts - representing four earthly kingdoms that would rise and fall. The last of these, the fourth beast, would be "dreadful and terrible" and would "devour the whole earth."

This beast, Daniel wrote, "shall speak pompous words against the Most High, shall persecute the saints of the Most High, and shall intend to change times and law." Sound familiar?

The Isaiah Warning The book of Isaiah also offers a chilling prophecy for our times. In chapter 24, the prophet warns that the "earth is also defiled under its inhabitants" and that the "curse has devoured the earth." He describes a world of lawlessness, where "the earth is also polluted by its inhabitants."

Isaiah's words ring true as we witness the breakdown of law and order, the rampant disregard for human life, and the corruption that has infected the highest levels of government.

The Prophetic Timeline Brothers and sisters, the time of tribulation is upon us. The Book of Revelation lays out the sequence of events that will unfold - the four horsemen, the seven seals, the bowl judgments. And time and again, we see these prophecies coming to pass.

We are living in the end times, my friends. The signs are all there, plain as day. We must open our eyes and heed the warnings of Scripture, for the time of reckoning is at hand.
