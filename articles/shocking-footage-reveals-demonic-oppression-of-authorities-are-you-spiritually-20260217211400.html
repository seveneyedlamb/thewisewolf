The Shocking Truth Behind the Execution Caught on Camera

This is not just a case of police brutality. The disturbing footage obtained by The Wise Wolf shows a disturbing pattern of demonic influence and spiritual warfare playing out on our city streets.

In the video, half a dozen ICE agents are seen brutally beating a handcuffed man who is lying face-down on the ground. Suddenly, one of the agents draws his weapon and fires five shots point-blank into the restrained man as a bystander runs away in terror.

Federal authorities are already spinning this as a justified use of force, claiming the man was "armed and dangerous." But the video proves they are lying - the man posed zero threat, his hands cuffed behind his back. This was an cold-blooded execution, plain and simple.

The Battle in the Heavenly Realms

Yet as Christians, we must look beyond the physical realm to discern what is truly happening here. The Apostle Paul warned us that "our struggle is not against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the authorities, against the powers of this dark world and against the spiritual forces of evil in the heavenly realms" (Ephesians 6:12).

This shocking act of violence is not just an isolated incident of police brutality. It is a manifestation of the unseen spiritual war raging all around us. The principalities and powers of darkness are actively working to oppress and persecute the people of God, using the very authorities tasked with maintaining order.

Are You Spiritually Prepared?

Just as the prophet Daniel was thrown into the lions' den, the righteous in our day are being targeted and silenced. This is a sobering wake-up call for all believers - the time for spiritual complacency is over. We must put on the full armor of God (Ephesians 6:10-18) and be prepared to stand firm against the schemes of the devil.

The days grow darker. The spiritual warfare more intense. But take heart, for our Lord has overcome the world (John 16:33). If we remain steadfast in faith, no weapon formed against us shall prosper (Isaiah 54:17).

Are you spiritually ready for what is to come? The time is now to strengthen your relationship with Christ and gird yourself for the battles ahead.
