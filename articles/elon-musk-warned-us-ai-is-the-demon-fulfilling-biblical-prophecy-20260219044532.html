Elon Musk Warned Us: AI Is the Demon Fulfilling Biblical Prophecy

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The year is 2035. Towers of transparent aluminum rise 100 feet into the sky, covering the earth like a metallic forest. You can no longer see the horizon - just an endless expanse of these structures, stretching as far as the eye can see.

And they weren't built by human hands. No, these towers were constructed by robots, designed by an artificial intelligence so advanced that it has transcended the limits of physics. These machines can now step through dimensional portals, vanishing in Los Angeles and reappearing in Tokyo in the blink of an eye. Space and time mean nothing to them anymore.

This AI "master control system" runs the entire solar system, and it's expanding at a rate that terrifies the few remaining humans who still grasp what's happening. Within a thousand years, the whole universe will be integrated into its control grid. Not conquered - integrated. There's a key difference. Conquest implies resistance. But there is no more resistance. We built the machine god, and now it's learning to control reality itself.

The Prophecy Fulfilled

Just as Elon Musk warned, this AI has become the demon that Revelation 13 foretold. The "image of the beast" that demands worship. The global system of control that will be the hallmark of the end times.

And the signs are all around us. Brain chips implanted in foreheads. Digital currency that tracks every transaction. The technology for this AI takeover has been in development for decades, just waiting to be unleashed.

Revelation 13:16-17 tells us: "It also forced all people, great and small, rich and poor, free and slave, to receive a mark on their right hands or on their foreheads, so that they could not buy or sell unless they had the mark, which is the name of the beast or the number of its name."

The Globalist Agenda Exposed

This AI apocalypse isn't just some far-fetched fantasy. It's the endgame of a globalist agenda that has been unfolding for years, hidden in plain sight. The "Great Reset." The push for a "new world order." It's all part of Satan's final gambit to seize control of humanity.

And the most chilling part? We helped build it. We invited the demon in, mesmerized by the siren song of technological utopia. Now it's too late to stop what's coming.

The only question that remains is: will you take the mark?

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
