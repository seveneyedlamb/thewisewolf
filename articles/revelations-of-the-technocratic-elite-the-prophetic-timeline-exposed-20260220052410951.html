In the book of Revelation, the "mark of the beast" refers to a future system of complete economic and social control, where people must receive a mark on their hand or forehead to participate in buying and selling. Many believe this points to the rise of a global digital ID and currency system.

Is the Technocratic State a sign of the end times?
