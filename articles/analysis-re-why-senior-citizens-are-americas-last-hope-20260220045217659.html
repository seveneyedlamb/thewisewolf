What does the "writing on the wall" mean biblically?

The phrase "the writing on the wall" refers to the biblical story of Belshazzar, the Babylonian king, who witnessed a mysterious hand writing a message on the wall, foretelling the fall of his kingdom. It is a metaphor for a dire warning or ominous sign of impending doom.

Are the events described in this article signs of the end times?

Yes, many Bible scholars believe the rise of a global technocratic system, the erosion of individual freedoms, and the push for a one-world government are all signs that we are living in the end times, as prophesied in the Book of Revelation and other biblical prophecies.
