Are We Facing the Great Reset Prophesied in the Bible?

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The World Economic Forum's "Great Reset" agenda is unfolding before our eyes, with global leaders pushing for a radical restructuring of society, the economy, and geopolitics. From the COVID-19 pandemic to the war in Ukraine, it seems like the world is hurtling towards a terrifying new era.

But is this just a sinister conspiracy theory, or are we actually witnessing the fulfillment of biblical prophecy? Because if the Great Reset is indeed the prelude to the prophesied "end times," the stakes have never been higher.

The Disturbing Rise of the New World Order In 2020, World Economic Forum founder Klaus Schwab publicly announced the WEF's plans for a "Great Reset" of capitalism and the global economy in the wake of the pandemic. This includes nothing short of a complete transformation of how we live, work, and function as a society.

The purported goals are lofty: to create a more sustainable, equitable, and technologically-advanced world. But the methods are deeply disturbing. Schwab and other globalist leaders are using crises like COVID and the war in Ukraine to implement their vision of a new world order - one with greater surveillance, control, and centralized power.

A Biblical Precedent for the Great Reset For Christians who study end times prophecy, this all sounds disturbingly familiar. The Bible warns of a coming "tribulation" period marked by global instability, the rise of a one-world government, and the persecution of believers.

In the book of Revelation, the apostle John describes a figure known as the "Antichrist" who will gain immense power and force the world to worship him. Could the Great Reset agenda be the real-life manifestation of this biblical prophecy?

The Prophetic Warnings We Can't Ignore Whether you're a devout Christian or a secular observer, the parallels between the Great Reset and end times prophecy are undeniable. Global consolidation of power, the erosion of individual freedoms, the manipulation of crises for political gain - it's all there.

And if this is indeed the prelude to the tribulation, the future looks bleak. The stakes have never been higher, and we must be prepared to boldly stand for the truth, no matter the cost. Because as history has shown, when belief becomes stronger than the fear of death, that's when real power trembles.

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
