The hour is late, my fellow believers. The darkness gathers, and the light of truth grows dim. Like a watchman on the wall, I sound the alarm - the end times are here. The signs are all around us, screaming the imminent return of our Lord and Savior.

The Tribulation Approaches The events unfolding in Minneapolis this weekend are a chilling harbinger of what is to come. This is merely a taste of the tyranny and deception that will consume the world in the days ahead.

The Mark of the Beast Rises The technology to implement the dreaded "mark of the beast" system described in the book of Revelation is already here. Microchips that can be implanted in the forehead or hand, tied to a global digital currency that tracks every transaction. "No one can buy or sell unless he has the mark" (Revelation 13:17).

The godless globalists are maneuvering to usher in this system of total control. As the apostle Paul warned, "The man of lawlessness is revealed, the son of destruction" (2 Thessalonians 2:3). This is no longer speculation - the end times are unfolding before our eyes.

Watchmen, Sound the Alarm Church, it is time to awaken from our slumber. The midnight hour approaches, and the world hurtles toward the final confrontation between good and evil. Will you be found faithful when the Lord returns? Or will you be swept away in the great deception?

"Therefore keep watch, because you do not know the day or the hour" (Matthew 25:13). Heed this urgent warning, my brothers and sisters. The Day of the Lord is at hand. Repent, believe the gospel, and be ready.
