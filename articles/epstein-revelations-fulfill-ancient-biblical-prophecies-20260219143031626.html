## Warnings From the Prophets

The prophet Daniel foresaw this moment over 2,500 years ago. In his apocalyptic visions, he described a future "king" who would "do as he pleases" and "exalt and magnify himself above every god." (Daniel 11:36) Sound familiar?

## The Mark of the Beast

But it gets worse. The prophet John in the Book of Revelation foretold a coming "beast" system that would force everyone to receive a "mark" in order to buy or sell. (Revelation 13:16-17) Some scholars believe this refers to a global cashless currency controlled by an elite few.

Epstein was deeply involved in developing this new digital economy, including his connections to cryptocurrency and Internet of Things technology. The ability to track every transaction is the perfect setup for the "mark of the beast" prophesied in Scripture.

The Epstein scandal is a dark sign of the times. It reveals the depth of evil infecting the highest levels of power. As the prophet Isaiah warned, "Woe to those who call evil good and good evil, who put darkness for light and light for darkness." (Isaiah 5:20)
