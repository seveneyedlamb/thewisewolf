The Devil's Bank Account: Jeffrey Epstein and the Satanic Ritual Abuse of Children

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The technology exists right now. Brain chips go in your forehead. Digital currency tracks every transaction. Revelation 13 warned us 2000 years ago.

And yet, the elites continue their dark rituals, exploiting the vulnerable and sacrificing the innocent. The case of Jeffrey Epstein is a chilling reminder that the forces of evil are alive and well, operating in the shadows of our society.

Epstein, the disgraced financier who died in prison under mysterious circumstances, was no ordinary criminal. His elaborate web of sex trafficking and child abuse was fueled by a disturbing obsession with the occult - an obsession that went all the way to the top.

According to recently released DOJ files, Epstein named one of his bank accounts after "Moloch," a Canaanite deity associated with child sacrifice. This is no mere coincidence. It is a clear and unambiguous reference to the satanic practices that lay at the heart of Epstein's operation.

The Bible prophesied this very scenario thousands of years ago. In the book of Revelation, we are told of a "beast" that will rise to power, wielding control over the world's financial systems and demanding worship from the masses. This beast, the Antichrist, will stop at nothing to secure his grip on humanity, even if it means the systematic abuse and murder of children.

The parallels between Epstein's crimes and the biblical prophecies are chilling. From the use of "Moloch" as a code name to the involvement of powerful political and social elites, the evidence suggests that Epstein was part of a vast, well-organized satanic network - one that has been operating in the shadows for centuries.

And the corruption goes even deeper. We now know that Epstein had close ties to former President Donald Trump, as well as to the Clintons and other high-profile figures. The question is: how many of our leaders are complicit in these heinous acts?

As Christians, we must not shy away from the truth. The battle against evil is real, and it is being waged on the highest levels of power. The Epstein scandal is not just a story of one man's depravity, but a window into a much larger, more sinister reality.

The time has come to shine the light of truth on the darkness. We must demand justice, not just for the victims of Epstein's crimes, but for all those who have suffered at the hands of the satanic elite. And we must trust in the power of God's word, for it is the only thing that can truly overcome the forces of evil that threaten to engulf our world.
