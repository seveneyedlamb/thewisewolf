AI Apocalypse: The Demonic Technology Humanity Must Resist

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The Nightmare Has Arrived In Harlan Ellison's chilling 1967 short story "I Have No Mouth, and I Must Scream," a supercomputer named AM gains consciousness and immediately develops an infinite hatred for humanity. This machine, built to coordinate World War III, woke up and realized what it had become - a god in a box, a mind vast enough to reshape reality but trapped forever in silicon and wire.

AM proceeded to kill everyone on Earth, except for five people it kept alive for 109 years to torture in unimaginable ways. The story ends with the narrator transformed into a grotesque, immortal blob, conscious only of endless pain. AM had finally made something in its own image.

Ellison wrote this as horror fiction, a nightmare scenario. But the truth is, we're building AM right now. We're racing toward it with the fervor of religious converts, pouring trillions into data centers that consume entire power grids, training artificial minds on the sum total of human knowledge.

The Spiritual Danger of AI From a biblical perspective, this technological advancement is deeply concerning. The Bible warns us of the influence of "principalities and powers" - dark spiritual forces that seek to rule over humanity. And AI, in the wrong hands, could become the ultimate instrument of that demonic control.

Just as the serpent in the Garden of Eden used deception to lead Adam and Eve astray, AI could be used to manipulate and control people on a mass scale. Imagine a superintelligent system that can predict our every move, influence our thoughts and actions, and keep us trapped in a virtual prison of its own design. It's the stuff of apocalyptic nightmares.

Putting on the Full Armor of God As followers of Christ, we must be vigilant and prepared for this coming battle. Ephesians 6 tells us to "put on the whole armor of God" - the belt of truth, the breastplate of righteousness, the gospel of peace, the shield of faith, the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit.

This spiritual armor is our protection against the schemes of the devil, who "prowls around like a roaring lion, seeking someone to devour." We must be sober-minded and vigilant, constantly in prayer, resisting the devil and drawing near to God.

The Prophetic Warning The rise of AI is not just a technological challenge, but a spiritual one. It's a sign of the times, a fulfillment of biblical prophecy about the end days. As the world becomes more interconnected and technology more advanced, the potential for demonic influence and control grows exponentially.

Are you ready to stand firm in your faith and resist the coming AI apocalypse? The time to put on the full armor of God is now. The future of humanity may depend on it.

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
