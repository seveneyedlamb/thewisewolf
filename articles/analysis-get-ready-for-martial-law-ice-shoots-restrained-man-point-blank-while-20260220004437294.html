Analysis: Get Ready for Martial Law: ICE Shoots Restrained Man Point-Blank While Bystander Runs Screaming

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

I was minding my own business the other day, sipping my coffee and scanning the headlines, when I nearly spit that caffeine all over my screen. There it was - a story about federal agents executing a handcuffed man in broad daylight. Right in the middle of Minneapolis. And the mainstream media is trying to cover it up.

Now, I know what you're thinking. "Here goes that crazy Christian guy again, rambling about prophecies and the end of the world." But hear me out, because this isn't some tinfoil hat nonsense. This is straight-up biblical.

The Daniel Prophecy

The prophet Daniel foresaw all of this back in the 6th century BC. He described a series of world empires that would rise and fall, culminating in a final global superpower - a "beast" that would seek to control every aspect of life. Does that sound familiar?

We're talking surveillance, digital currency, mandatory "marks" - the whole nine yards. And Daniel said this beast would ruthlessly persecute and execute anyone who refuses to worship it. Sound like the video we just saw in Minneapolis?

The Isaiah Warning

But it gets worse. The prophet Isaiah also warned of a time when "darkness will cover the earth and deep darkness the peoples." He described a world where "the wicked will do wickedly, and none of the wicked will understand." Folks, I think we're living in that world.

The Tribulation Timeline

According to Scripture, these are the birth pangs of the tribulation - the tumultuous prelude to Christ's return and the establishment of His eternal kingdom. Signs like economic collapse, natural disasters, and government tyranny are all part of the prophetic timeline.

And what did Jesus say? "When you see these things happening, know that the kingdom of God is near." Well, folks, I think the kingdom is knocking on the door. Are you ready?
