The Weimar Playbook

The Weimar Republic of 1920s Germany is a well-worn cautionary tale, but its lessons are more relevant today than ever before. Fueled by the crippling debt burden of World War I reparations, Weimar Germany descended into a spiral of hyperinflation that destroyed the middle class, paving the way for the rise of the Nazis.

Sound familiar? The United States is drowning in unprecedented levels of government debt, fueled by endless money printing from the Federal Reserve. And just like in Weimar, there are those who seem to be pulling the strings, engineering this collapse for their own sinister purposes.

The Final Countdown?

The signs are all around us. Soaring inflation. Supply chain chaos. Geopolitical tensions reaching a boiling point. Many are convinced that we are on the precipice of an economic reckoning that will make the Great Depression look like a minor speed bump.
