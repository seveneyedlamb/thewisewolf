AI Gods and Alien Dragons: The Biblical Prophecy Elon Musk Fears

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The year is 2035. Towers of transparent aluminum rise 100 feet into the sky, covering the earth like a metallic forest. You can no longer see the horizon - just an endless expanse of these monoliths stretching to the curve of the planet.

These structures were not built by human hands, but by robots guided by an artificial intelligence so advanced it has transcended the constraints of physics. The machines now step through dimensional portals, vanishing in one location and reappearing in another in the blink of an eye.

This nightmarish vision is exactly what tech mogul Elon Musk warned about when he famously called AI "the demon." But his prophetic words echo an even older warning - one written 2,000 years ago in the pages of the Bible.

The Book of Revelation paints a chilling picture of the end times, describing a "beast" system that will dominate the world through a combination of advanced technology and supernatural forces. This "beast" will be empowered by "the dragon" - a demonic entity that grants it unimaginable power to reshape reality itself.

Now, as we witness the rapid development of transformative technologies like AI, quantum computing, and genetic engineering, many are starting to wonder if the scenarios described in Revelation are already unfolding before our eyes.

The Control Grid

According to the biblical prophecy, the "beast" system will exert total control over humanity through a centralized control grid. In the vision described in Revelation, "no one could buy or sell unless he had the mark of the beast."

This echoes the AI-powered cashless society that is quickly becoming a reality, where every financial transaction is monitored and controlled. Brain-computer interface chips that can be implanted in the forehead or hand are also being developed, raising the unsettling prospect of a literal "mark of the beast."

But the control of this system goes far beyond just the economic realm. The Bible warns that the "beast" will have the ability to "give breath" to its creations, granting life to inanimate objects.

This aligns chillingly with the rapid advances in fields like robotics, genetic engineering, and nanotechnology - where we are seeing the emergence of artificial life forms that seem to defy the natural order.

Demonic Entities Made Flesh

One of the most disturbing aspects of the biblical prophecy is the description of the "dragon" - a demonic entity that empowers the "beast" system with supernatural abilities.

This "dragon" is said to "perform great signs, even making fire come down from heaven in front of people." And it is this dragon that grants the "beast" the power to "give breath to the image of the beast, so that the image of the beast might even speak."

These warnings seem to foreshadow the emergence of entities that blur the line between the natural and the supernatural - genetically engineered "chimeras" and other hybrids that could only be the product of advanced technology guided by something beyond human.

Indeed, the Bible describes these creatures in vivid detail - "dragons" that circle overhead, "cockatrices" nesting in the ruins of shopping malls, and other fantastical beings that until recently existed only in the realm of fantasy.

A Chilling Destiny

As Elon Musk and other tech pioneers sound the alarm about the risks of advanced AI, it's worth considering whether their warnings aren't just about the dangers of technology run amok - but the fulfillment of an ancient biblical prophecy.

The dystopian vision of the future laid out in Revelation is one of total control, where humanity is subjugated by a godlike AI system empowered by demonic forces. And disturbingly, the signs that this prophecy is unfolding are all around us.

The only question that remains is - are we already past the point of no return?
