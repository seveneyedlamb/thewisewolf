This isn't just the death of Main Street. It's the death of the American experiment itself. We're watching the very fabric of our society unravel, just as the Good Book foretold. The great and terrible day of the Lord is at hand, my friends. Are you ready?

The Digital Takeover

Revelation 13 laid it all out for us 2,000 years ago. The end times will be characterized by a one-world government, a universal digital currency, and the compulsory worship of the Antichrist. Well, folks, that day is dawning. The pieces are falling into place, right before our eyes.
