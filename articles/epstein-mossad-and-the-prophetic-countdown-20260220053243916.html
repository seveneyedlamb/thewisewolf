Epstein, Mossad, and the Prophetic Countdown

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The stench of corruption hangs thick in the air, like the sulfurous fog of Dante's Inferno. As an investigative journalist, I've seen my fair share of twisted, nightmarish tales. But the Jeffrey Epstein saga is one for the books - a sordid, tangled web that reaches all the way to the highest echelons of power...and the very heart of biblical prophecy.

Epstein, a convicted sex offender with powerful connections, was secretly running a surveillance company called Carbyne. And get this - his co-founders were none other than Peter Thiel, Elon Musk's billionaire buddy, and former Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Barak.

Biblical Breadcrumbs

What's the connection between a perverse pedophile, a tech oligarch, and the former leader of Israel? Well, my friend, the answers lie in the pages of Scripture.

The Prophet Daniel Warned Us

Over 2,500 years ago, the prophet Daniel had a vision of the End Times. He described a coming "beast" system that would seek to control all of humanity. This beast, he wrote, would "wear out the saints of the Most High" and "think to change the times and the law" (Daniel 7:25).

Does that sound familiar? Epstein's Carbyne company can monitor 911 calls, turning on people's cameras and accessing their GPS. It's staffed by former operatives of Unit 8200 - Israel's version of the NSA. In other words, a convicted sex offender, a surveillance capitalist, and the former head of Israel teamed up to create a system that can watch, track, and potentially persecute the faithful.

The Tribulation Cometh

The prophet Isaiah also warned of a time when "darkness shall cover the earth, and deep darkness the peoples" (Isaiah 60:2). Epstein's shadowy network, with its ties to Mossad and the Israeli government, could be a chilling sign of the tribulation to come.

As the Revelation of John foretold, a one-world system will emerge, complete with a mark on the forehead or hand to participate in the economy (Revelation 13:16-17). Sound familiar? Brain chips and digital currency, anyone?

The clock is ticking, my friends. The forces of darkness are aligning. Are you ready for what's about to unfold?
