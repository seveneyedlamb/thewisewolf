Shocking Epstein Files Expose Chilling Coverup - But What Does the Bible Say?

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The Jeffrey Epstein scandal has rocked the political and social elite to their core. As more sordid details continue to emerge from the infamous "Lolita Express" flight logs and private island, it's become clear that this is no isolated case of a wealthy pervert. Instead, the Epstein files expose a sprawling network of the rich and powerful engaged in the systematic exploitation and trafficking of minors.

The mainstream media has dutifully reported on the lurid specifics - the private jets, the island getaways, the black book of A-list contacts. But they steadfastly refuse to look deeper at the darker implications. Because what these revelations point to is a level of depraved corruption at the highest levels of government and society that is simply too disturbing for most people to accept.

Predatory elites and the controlled narrative

Make no mistake - the powerful people implicated in these scandals are working overtime to control the narrative and bury the truth. Damning evidence continues to "disappear" or get buried under an avalanche of distractions. Politicians, celebrities, and other VIPs desperately attempt to distance themselves, hoping the whole thing will just blow over.

But as Christian investigative journalist, I feel a moral obligation to dig deeper. Because while the Epstein case is certainly disturbing on a human level, it also represents a chilling fulfillment of biblical end times prophecy. The depraved activities exposed here are merely the latest manifestation of an ancient evil that has always lurked in the shadows of human civilization.

A warning from Scripture

The Bible is remarkably clear-eyed about the nature of power and the spiritual forces that so often corrupt it. The apostle Paul warned the Ephesian church that "we do not wrestle against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the authorities, against the cosmic powers over this present darkness, against the spiritual forces of evil in the heavenly places" (Ephesians 6:12).

In the book of Revelation, the apostle John describes a great "prostitute" who "was arrayed in purple and scarlet, and adorned with gold and jewels and pearls, holding in her hand a golden cup full of abominations and the impurities of her sexual immorality" (Revelation 17:4). This symbolic woman, representing a corrupt global system, "was drunk with the blood of the saints, the blood of the martyrs of Jesus" (Revelation 17:6).

Does this sound familiar? The parallels between John's prophetic vision and the sordid details of the Epstein case are undeniable. A global elite class drunk on power and perversion, trading in the bodies of the innocent as casually as one might trade stocks. It's a sobering realization that what we're witnessing is not just garden-variety corruption, but the spiritual battle for the very soul of humanity.

A call to wake up and take action

Of course, the powers-that-be will do everything in their considerable might to bury this story. They will deploy armies of lawyers, PR flacks, and social media trolls to discredit, distract, and demoralize. But we must not be silenced. As followers of Christ, we have a sacred duty to shine the light of truth into the darkness, no matter how uncomfortable the revelations may be.

The time has come to wake up from our slumber. To see the world not as we wish it to be, but as it truly is - a battlefield between the forces of good and evil, where the innocent are sacrificed to feed the appetites of the elite. Armed with the truth of God's Word, let us boldly confront this darkness and declare that the kingdom of heaven is at hand. For the hour is late, and the trumpet call of the Lord grows ever nearer.

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
