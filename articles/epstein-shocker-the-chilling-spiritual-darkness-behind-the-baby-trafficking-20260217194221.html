The Demonic Fingerprints

When you scratch the surface, the fingerprints of the demonic are all over this scandal. Occult symbols, ritual abuse, and connections to dark spiritual forces pervade the Epstein case. This wasn't just wealthy deviants indulging their carnal desires - this was spiritual warfare being waged against the very fabric of God's creation.

As Christians, we must understand that this isn't just a matter of catching a few bad actors. The Epstein files reveal a deep, systemic corruption fueled by principalities and powers in the spiritual realm. These are the tactics of the Enemy - to steal, kill and destroy, using the wealthy and powerful as his willing instruments.

A Call to Spiritual Arms

The Epstein leaks should serve as a wake-up call to the church. We are engaged in a battle not against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the authorities, against the powers of this dark world and against the spiritual forces of evil in the heavenly realms. (Ephesians 6:12)

Now, more than ever, it is time to put on the full armor of God. We must be vigilant in prayer, steadfast in our faith, and uncompromising in our stance against the forces of darkness. For our struggle is not against people, but against the spiritual forces of evil that seek to devour us.
