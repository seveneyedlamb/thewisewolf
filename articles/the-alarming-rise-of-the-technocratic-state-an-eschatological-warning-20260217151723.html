The Alarming Rise of the Technocratic State: An Eschatological Warning

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The Technocratic State is on the Rise

My fellow Christians, we are living in a time of unprecedented spiritual warfare. The powers of darkness are waging a relentless assault on our freedoms, our values, and our very way of life. At the forefront of this onslaught is the ever-expanding Technocratic State - a sinister alliance of big tech, big government, and global elites hellbent on total control.

A Desperate Plea for Help

A subscriber recently reached out to me, voicing a sentiment that I'm sure resonates with many of you: "I'm fully awake. My red pill moment was after 9/11 realizing our own government attacked us. But besides speaking up, I feel like the Technocratic State is taking control faster than anything I can do."

This is the cry of the remnant - those who have had their eyes opened to the truth, but feel powerless in the face of such overwhelming darkness. It's a cry that echoes through the pages of Scripture, from the prophets of old to the apostles of the early Church.

The Last Stand of the Seniors

But there may be a glimmer of hope, and it lies in an unlikely demographic: our senior citizens. As I told the subscriber, the only thing we can do right now is wait and prepare. We need 100,000+ senior citizens to march on Washington.

Why? Because this is the one group the Technocratic State cannot easily silence or discredit. There is no way to spin an 80-year-old grandmother into a violent extremist. There is no way to pepper spray a Vietnam veteran without sparking a full-scale revolt. And there is no way to deploy ICE or National Guard troops against your own parents and grandparents without the whole country turning on you.

Seniors are the last Americans left with the moral authority to protest without being labeled as "domestic terrorists" or "insurrectionists." They are the final bulwark against the encroaching darkness - the generation that has seen it all and is not afraid to stand up for what's right.

A Prophetic Call to Action

My friends, this is a critical moment in the history of our nation, and perhaps even in the unfolding of God's eternal plan. The Technocratic State is rising, but the power of the Holy Spirit is greater. And if we can rally our senior saints to take a stand, we may just be able to stem the tide and buy ourselves some precious time.

The clock is ticking, and the signs of the times are all around us. It's time to heed the call of the prophets and prepare for the days ahead. Will you answer the challenge?

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Technocratic State from a biblical perspective?

The Technocratic State is a term used to describe the growing alliance between big tech, big government, and global elites who seek to exert total control over society. Biblically, this can be seen as a manifestation of the end-times spirit of Antichrist, as described in passages like Revelation 13.

Are the events described in this article signs of the end times?

While we must be careful about making definitive claims, the rise of the Technocratic State and the erosion of freedoms can certainly be interpreted as prophetic warning signs. Passages like 2 Timothy 3 and Matthew 24 describe a time of increasing wickedness and tyranny leading up to the return of Christ.
