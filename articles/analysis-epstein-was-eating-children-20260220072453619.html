Revelation Unfolding

This should be a wakeup call to every Christian. The Bible warned us about this. Will the Church Stand Firm?

Believers, we are the watchmen on the wall. Will we have the courage to sound the alarm and expose this evil? Or will we shrink back in fear, hoping it all goes away? The choice is ours. But make no mistake - the time for complacency is over.
