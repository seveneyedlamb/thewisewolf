A Shocking Showdown Looms

The stage is set for a dramatic confrontation. On one side, you have the all-powerful Technocratic elite, desperate to solidify their control. On the other, you have the moral authority of America's seniors, the last line of defense against tyranny.

What happens when these two forces collide? The outcome could shock the world and change the course of history. Biblical prophecy is unfolding right before our eyes. And the seniors may be the key to stopping it.
