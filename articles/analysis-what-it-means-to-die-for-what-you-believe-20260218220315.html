The globalists have a plan, and it's not for your benefit. They're orchestrating a worldwide economic reset that will pave the way for a new world order under a single governing authority. All in the name of "safety" and "equality," of course. But the truth is far more sinister.

The Coming Tribulation If you think the COVID-19 pandemic was bad, just wait. The Bible warns of a time of unparalleled trouble and suffering to come - the Great Tribulation. Wars, famines, pestilences, and natural disasters on a scale never before seen. And at the center of it all, a evil world leader who will demand worship and control every aspect of life.

Is this all just speculation and fear-mongering? Hardly. The evidence is mounting, the puzzle pieces are falling into place. The question is, will you have eyes to see the truth before it's too late?

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the "Mark of the Beast" mean biblically?

The "Mark of the Beast" refers to a prophesied global system of control and surveillance, where people will be required to receive a mark on their hand or forehead in order to buy, sell, or participate in society. This is seen as a sign of allegiance to the Antichrist and the end-times global government.

Are current events a sign of the end times?

Many Bible-believing Christians believe the rapid technological, political, and social changes we're witnessing are clear fulfillments of end-times prophecy. Things like the push for a global digital currency, the erosion of personal freedoms, and the rise of authoritarian control all point to the imminent establishment of the Antichrist's kingdom.
