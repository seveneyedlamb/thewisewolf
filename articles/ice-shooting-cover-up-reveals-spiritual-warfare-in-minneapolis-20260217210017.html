ICE Shooting Cover-Up Reveals Spiritual Warfare in Minneapolis

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

On Saturday morning in Minneapolis, federal agents from Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) brutally attacked a Christian nurse named Alex Pretti. Eyewitnesses say the 37-year-old ICU worker at the Veterans Affairs Medical Center was simply trying to help a woman who had been pepper-sprayed. But instead of receiving aid, Pretti was surrounded by agents, beaten, and shot multiple times. He died at the hospital.

Now the Department of Homeland Security is trying to cover up the truth. Secretary Kristi Noem brazenly lied to the public, claiming that Pretti approached the officers "menacingly with a semi-automatic handgun with dozens of rounds of ammunition." But as any gun owner knows, you can't SEE "dozens of rounds" inside a closed magazine. This blatant falsehood is a clear attempt to demonize the victim and justify the agents' actions.

We are not wrestling against flesh and blood, but against the rulers, against the authorities, against the cosmic powers over this present darkness, against the spiritual forces of evil in the heavenly places. (Ephesians 6:12)

This isn't an isolated incident. Over the past year, we've seen a coordinated effort by government officials and the media to demonize and persecute Christians, spreading lies and sowing division. From the January 6th witch hunt to the censorship of pastors, there is a clear spiritual battle unfolding before our eyes.

**A Prophetic Sign of the End Times**

The Bible warns that in the last days, believers will face increasing persecution and tribulation. "Indeed, all who desire to live a godly life in Christ Jesus will be persecuted." (2 Timothy 3:12) We are seeing the fulfillment of Jesus' prophecy that "you will be hated by all for my name's sake." (Matthew 10:22)

But take heart, for our Savior has overcome the world. Through the full armor of God, we can stand firm against the schemes of the devil. (Ephesians 6:10-18) This is a call to spiritual warfare - to pray without ceasing, to put on the breastplate of righteousness, and to wield the sword of the Spirit.

The truth will ultimately prevail, for "the light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it." (John 1:5) Stay vigilant, my fellow believers. The battle rages, but our King is coming soon.
