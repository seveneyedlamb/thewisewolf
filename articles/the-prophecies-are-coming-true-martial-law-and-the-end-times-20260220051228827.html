The Prophecies Are Coming True: Martial Law and the End Times

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

As a worried parent, I can't help but feel a chill run down my spine every time I turn on the news. The signs of the times are undeniable. Martial law, global surveillance, and the rise of the beast system - it's all unfolding right before our eyes, just as the Bible foretold.

The Chilling Fulfillment of Daniel's Prophecy The recent incident in Minneapolis is a prime example. Federal agents mercilessly executed a restrained, handcuffed man on the street, and the mainstream media is covering it up. This isn't immigration enforcement - this is state-sanctioned murder. And it's straight out of the Book of Daniel.

Daniel 7 describes a "fourth beast, dreadful and terrible" that will "devour the whole earth." This beast represents a tyrannical global government that will persecute and kill God's people. The video from Minneapolis shows us the face of that beast. These federal agents are the enforcers of the coming new world order.

The Isaiah Warning And it doesn't stop there. The prophet Isaiah also had a chilling vision of our present reality: "The show of their countenance doth witness against them; and they declare their sin as Sodom, they hide it not" (Isaiah 3:9).

Just look around - sin and depravity are celebrated openly, while righteousness is mocked and suppressed. The world is racing towards the moral abyss, hurtling headlong into the tribulation foretold in the Book of Revelation.

The Revelation Timeline The end times clock is ticking, my friends. The technology for the mark of the beast is already here - brain chips, digital currency, total surveillance. Revelation 13 warned us 2000 years ago that a global beast system would arise, forcing everyone to pledge allegiance or be cut off from society.

Well, that system is being erected right before our eyes. The noose is tightening. And as followers of Christ, we must be ready. The prophecies are unfolding. Judgment is coming. The only question is, are you prepared?
