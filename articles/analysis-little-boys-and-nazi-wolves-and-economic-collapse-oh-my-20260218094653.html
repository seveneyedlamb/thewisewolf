"They never believed the boy when it mattered." This ancient parable about the boy who cried wolf holds a sobering warning for our times. Just as the villagers ignored the boy's frantic pleas about a real threat, many believers today are sleepwalking through the clear signs of an impending calamity.

The Parallels are Chilling The stage is set for the unfolding of end-times events prophesied in Scripture. Like the villagers, we've been desensitized by a constant barrage of sensational warnings that have not (yet) come to pass. But the wolf is at the door, my friends - and this time, the threat is all too real.

Global powers are engineering a orchestrated economic collapse, one designed to strip citizens of their freedoms and ushering in a new world order under the guise of a "Great Reset." Lockdowns, supply chain disruptions, and currency debasement are just the opening salvo. Soon, a global digital currency and social credit system will give the elite unprecedented control over our lives.

The Biblical Basis for the Great Reset This agenda aligns chillingly with biblical prophecies about the coming Tribulation and the rule of the Antichrist. Revelation 13 describes a global system where "no one may buy or sell" without submitting to the mark of the beast. Daniel 7 foretells of a global tyrant who "shall intend to change times and law."

The Watchman's Cry Like the shepherd boy, we must sound the alarm while there is still time. The world is racing toward a day of reckoning, and believers must be prepared - spiritually, emotionally, and practically. The signs are all around us. The wolves are at the door. Will you heed the warning?
