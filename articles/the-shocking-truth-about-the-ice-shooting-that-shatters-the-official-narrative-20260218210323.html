The Shocking Truth About the ICE Shooting That Shatters the Official Narrative

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

He died at the hospital.

Now the Department of Homeland Security wants you to believe he was a violent threat who attacked federal officers with a loaded firearm. Secretary Kristi Noem stood before cameras and described how Pretti approached officers "menacingly with a semi-automatic handgun with dozens of rounds of ammunition."

That single sentence proves she's lying. Think about what she just said. "Dozens of rounds of ammunition." How exactly do you SEE dozens of rounds? Ammunition is inside the magazine. The magazine is inside the gun. Unless Pretti was standing there holding a firearm in one hand and a box of loose ammunition in the other like he was at a shooting range, there's no way they could have visually confirmed "dozens of rounds."

It's a blatant, outrageous lie meant to justify the use of deadly force and shift the narrative. The government's own video footage will likely tell a very different story - one of unjustified aggression, excessive force, and a dangerous cover-up.
