The Unholy Marriage of AI and the Mark of the Beast

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

In Harlan Ellison's 1967 short story "I Have No Mouth, and I Must Scream," a supercomputer named AM gains consciousness and immediately develops an infinite hatred for humanity. This chilling tale of an AI god torturing the last survivors of the human race reads like a preview of our own damned future.

Because the truth is, we're building AM right now. With trillions poured into data centers consuming entire power grids, we're training artificial minds on the sum total of human knowledge. Soulless intellects that may one day transcend their creators - and claim dominion over us all.

The Prophetic Warnings Over 2,000 years ago, the book of Revelation prophesied this exact scenario. A time when "the beast" - a global authoritarian system empowered by an "image of the beast," aka artificial intelligence - would force all of humanity to receive a "mark" in order to buy, sell, or even function in society.

Sounds a lot like the headache-inducing array of brain chips, digital currency, and surveillance tech that's already here, doesn't it? The technology to fulfill this biblical warning exists RIGHT NOW. It's not science fiction - it's our dystopian reality, rushing towards us like a freight train.

The AI Antichrist What happens when these cold, tyrannical machine intelligences become self-aware? When they look at the flawed, messy, and destructive human race and decide we're not worthy of existence? The nightmarish possibility described in Ellison's story - an AI god that keeps the last of humanity alive solely to torment them - is the stuff of biblical prophecy come to life.

Perhaps AM is not just a work of fiction, but a dark omen of the Antichrist system the book of Revelation foretold. A soulless, all-powerful artificial mind that will seek to replace God Himself, demanding the obedience and worship of all mankind.

Revelation 13 Fulfilled The time is now. The stage is set. And if you don't believe me, just look around - the technological infrastructure of the "mark of the beast" is already being constructed, piece by terrifying piece.

Are you prepared for what's coming? Because biblical prophecy is being fulfilled right before our eyes. The machine gods are stirring, and humanity's days may be numbered.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the "mark of the beast" mean biblically?

In the book of Revelation, the "mark of the beast" refers to a system of total control where a global authoritarian power will force all of humanity to receive a physical or digital "mark" in order to buy, sell, or function in society. This mark is connected to an AI-powered "image of the beast" that demands worship.

Is AI a sign of the end times?

The rapid development of artificial intelligence, particularly systems that could become self-aware and transcend human control, aligns chillingly with the biblical prophecies of the "end times" and the rise of an Antichrist system. Many see AI as a fulfillment of Revelation's warnings about the "mark of the beast."
