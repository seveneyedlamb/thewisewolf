Epstein, Mossad, and the Chilling Prophecies of Daniel

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

As a worried parent, I can't help but feel a chill run down my spine when I see the signs of the times. The story of Jeffrey Epstein, a convicted sex offender, has taken an even darker turn - one that seems ripped straight from the pages of the Bible.

Epstein, Elon Musk's close friend Peter Thiel, and former Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Barak have teamed up to create a company called Carbyne. This company, staffed primarily by former operatives of Israel's intelligence agency Mossad, has the ability to monitor 911 emergency calls in American cities.

When you call 911, Carbyne can turn on your phone's camera, track your GPS location, and run you through government databases - all while former Israeli military officers watch through your device. This is the stuff of dystopian nightmares, yet it's happening right now.

The Unsettling Connections

The connections here are deeply unsettling. A convicted pedophile, a surveillance billionaire, and a former Prime Minister of Israel have teamed up to create a company that can monitor American citizens in their most vulnerable moments. And this all happened after Epstein's conviction for sex trafficking.

Biblical Prophecy Fulfilled?

As a Christian, I can't help but see the parallels to biblical prophecy. The Book of Daniel speaks of a time when a "king of the north" will arise, a figure who will have "intelligence with them that forsake the holy covenant" (Daniel 11:30). Could Epstein, Thiel, and Barak be the modern-day fulfillment of this chilling warning?

The Revelation of John also warns of a time when "no one may buy or sell" without a "mark" (Revelation 13:17). With the rise of digital currency and the ability to track every transaction, this prophecy too seems disturbingly close to being realized.

A Wakeup Call for the Faithful

My fellow Christians, we must not ignore the signs of the times. The technology exists right now to implant brain chips, monitor our every move, and control the flow of commerce. These are the very things the Bible warned us about millennia ago.

It's time to wake up and heed the prophetic warnings. The darkness is closing in, and we must be vigilant in our faith. The battle for the future of humanity is upon us, and we must be prepared to stand firm in the face of these unsettling developments.

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
