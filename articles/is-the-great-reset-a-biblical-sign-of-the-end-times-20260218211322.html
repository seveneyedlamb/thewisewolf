Is the Great Reset a Biblical Sign of the End Times?

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The world is changing faster than ever before. Totalitarian control. Surveillance states. Breakdown of traditional values. It's not just a leftist conspiracy theory - it's the Great Reset, a globalist plan to reshape the world economy and usher in a new era of technocratic control.

And Bible-believing Christians are sounding the alarm. Because when you line up the details of this New World Order with the book of Revelation, the connections are chilling.

The Bible warned us 2000 years ago about a coming global dictator, a one-world currency, and citizens being "marked" to participate in the economy. Now we're watching those ancient prophecies come to life right before our eyes.

Are we truly witnessing the birth pangs of the end times? A cursory glance at current events would suggest the answer is yes. From the engineered supply chain crisis to the aggressive push for digital IDs and programmable money - the scenario outlined in Scripture is unfolding with breathtaking speed.

Of course, the cynics will dismiss this as religious alarmism. But any honest student of history and geopolitics can see the ominous signs. Technocratic elites are consolidating power at a dizzying pace, using crises like COVID-19 to erode individual liberty and national sovereignty. The pieces of the end-times puzzle are falling into place.

So what does this mean for the body of Christ? It's a sobering reality check, to be sure. If the globalists have their way, believers may soon face the choice that martyrs throughout history have faced - deny your faith or face the consequences.

The hour is late. The stakes have never been higher. It's time to wake up, discern the signs of the times, and stand firm in the truth. Because as the ancient apostles discovered, sometimes the greatest victory lies in being willing to die for what you believe.
