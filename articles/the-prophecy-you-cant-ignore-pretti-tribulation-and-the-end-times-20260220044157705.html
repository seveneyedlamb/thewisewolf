The Prophecy You Can't Ignore: Pretti, Tribulation, and the End Times

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

My fellow believers, I come to you today with an urgent message. The signs of the end times are all around us, and we must open our eyes to the prophecies unfolding before our very eyes.

The Tragedy in Minneapolis

This past Saturday, a disturbing incident took place in Minneapolis that should send shudders down the spine of every Christian. Federal agents from the Department of Homeland Security confronted 37-year-old ICU nurse Alex Pretti, a veteran of the Veterans Affairs Medical Center. What happened next is the stuff of nightmares.

The agents pepper-sprayed a woman in the face at point-blank range. Pretti, seeing the injustice, stepped in to help. For his act of compassion, he was surrounded by multiple agents, beaten while on the ground, and then shot multiple times. He died at the hospital.

The government's story makes zero sense. They claim Pretti approached "menacingly with a semi-automatic handgun with dozens of rounds of ammunition." But how exactly does one "see" dozens of rounds? Ammunition is inside the magazine, inside the gun. There's no way the agents could have known the specifics of what Pretti was carrying. This reeks of a coverup.

The Daniel Prophecy

Friends, we must not ignore the prophetic implications of this tragic event. The prophet Daniel forewarned us of a time when "the people of the prince who is to come shall destroy the city and the sanctuary" (Daniel 9:26).

Could Pretti's murder at the hands of federal agents be a chilling preview of the tribulation to come? Daniel also prophesied that in the end times, "the people of the prince who is to come shall make war" and "shall destroy the holy people" (Daniel 11:31).

Revelation and the Mark of the Beast

And let us not forget the warnings of the book of Revelation. John the Apostle's apocalyptic vision foretold a time when "all, both small and great, both rich and poor, both free and slave, are forced to receive a mark on their right hand or on their forehead" (Revelation 13:16).

The technology for such a mark already exists - brain chips, digital currency, surveillance states. The pieces are falling into place, my friends. We must heed the biblical warnings and prepare our souls.

A Call to Action

The prophecies are unfolding before our eyes. Innocent blood spilled, the holy people under attack, the mark of the beast on the horizon. This is no time for apathy or complacency.

We must sound the alarm. We must awaken our fellow believers to the gravity of these End Times signs. The tribulation is coming - are you ready?

"The truth doesn't hide. It waits for those brave enough to look."
