A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The Epstein Revelations: A Prophetic Warning? According to the leaked files, Epstein's criminal enterprise went far beyond the sexual abuse of minors. Stunningly, there is evidence that he and his associates were actually selling babies on the black market. Yes, you read that right - selling innocent babies, some allegedly sourced from overseas, to the highest bidder.

This shocking revelation is a chilling fulfillment of biblical prophecy. The book of Revelation warns that in the "end times," the world will be plagued by the "trafficking of slaves and human souls" (Rev 18:13). Epstein's baby-selling operation seems to be a horrifying manifestation of this prophetic warning.

The Prophet Daniel's Grim Vision The prophet Daniel also foresaw a time when the "wicked" would gain power and "understand hidden things" in order to "corrupt" the innocent (Dan 8:23-25). Epstein's vast network of wealthy, influential contacts - from politicians to princes - suggests an ability to operate in the shadows and keep his twisted crimes hidden.

Worse yet, Daniel prophesied that this corrupt, all-powerful force would "destroy the mighty" and "destroy the holy people" - a grim prediction that seems to align with Epstein's targeting of children. As Christians, we must take these ancient warnings seriously.

A Wake-Up Call for the Church The Epstein leaks should serve as a wake-up call, reminding us that the "birth pains" of the end times are upon us (Matthew 24:8). These horrific revelations are not just a political scandal - they are a sign that the forces of evil are rapidly consolidating power, just as the Bible foretold.

The church must be vigilant and prepared to weather the coming storm. We must strengthen our faith, purify our hearts, and be ready to stand firm against the tide of darkness that threatens to engulf the world. The time for sleeping is over. God is calling us to be watchmen on the walls.
