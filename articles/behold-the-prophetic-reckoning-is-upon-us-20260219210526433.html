The Watchman's Warning

Brothers and sisters, the time for complacency is over. We are living in the last days, and the signs are all around us. Isaiah, Jeremiah, and John the Revelator - they all prophesied about these very events.

Will you heed the warnings of the watchmen on the wall? Or will you be caught unaware when the final tribulation begins?
