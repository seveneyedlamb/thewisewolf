Is the Minneapolis Shooting a Chilling Warning of End Times Deception?

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

On Saturday morning in Minneapolis, federal agents from Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) pepper sprayed a woman in the face at point blank range. Alex Pretti, a 37-year-old ICU nurse at the Veterans Affairs Medical Center, stepped in to help her. Multiple agents surrounded him, beat him while he was on the ground, and shot him multiple times. He died at the hospital.

Now the Department of Homeland Security wants you to believe he was a violent threat who attacked federal officers with a loaded firearm. But the facts just don't add up.

Secretary Kristi Noem stood before cameras and described how Pretti approached officers "menacingly with a semi-automatic handgun with dozens of rounds of ammunition." That single sentence proves she's lying. How exactly can you SEE dozens of rounds? Ammunition is inside the magazine, which is inside the gun. Unless Pretti was standing there holding a firearm in one hand and a box of loose ammunition in the other, there's no way the agents could have known the exact number of rounds.
