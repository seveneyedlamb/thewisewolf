But this isn't just some economic trend we're witnessing. No, my friends, this is the fulfillment of a prophecy laid out in the book of Revelation. Just look at the technology they're pushing. Brain chips, digital currency, surveillance on every transaction. It's all there, right in front of our eyes. Revelation 13 foretold of a time when people would be forced to receive a "mark" on their forehead or hand in order to participate in the economy. And that time, my friends, is now.

## Heed the Warning

I know it's easy to bury our heads in the sand, to pretend like none of this is real. But the truth is, the clock is ticking. The prophecies are unfolding before our very eyes, and if we don't wake up and fight back, we're going to lose everything that makes this country great.

So I'm calling on you, my fellow believers, to open your eyes and see the signs. Pray, prepare, and get ready, because the tribulation is coming. And if we don't stand up now, the soul of our nation will be consumed, just like the small towns that have fallen to the corporate beast.
