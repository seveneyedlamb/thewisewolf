The Epstein Files Expose a Satanic Network of Darkness

A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

The mainstream is treating the Epstein files like a celebrity gossip story. But Christians must see it for what it really is - a window into a Satanic underworld of child sacrifice and ritual abuse.

Somewhere between the mountains of evidence, it becomes clear that this is not simply a case of "sex trafficking." Criminals don't build temples, name bank accounts after Canaanite gods, or develop a systematic approach to the spiritual effects of raping a child.

What the Epstein files describe is a network of sorcerers engaged in dark, occult rituals to gain power and influence. They use crime, deception and depravity as a way to fund their twisted religious agenda. This is spiritual warfare at the highest levels.

The Temples of Baal Hidden on Epstein's private island are the signs of an elaborate pagan temple complex. Two large bronze pillars stand at the entrance, mirroring the "Jachin and Boaz" pillars from Solomon's Temple. The central chamber contains a stone altar, ritual cleaning baths, and crytps beneath the floor.

Nearby, Epstein maintained a bank account literally named "Gratitude Final" - an obvious reference to the Canaanite god Baal. These are not the signs of a simple sex trafficking operation. This is a temple for dark, supernatural worship.

The Staging of Spiritual Abuse Even more disturbing are the clinical notes found describing the "spiritual effects" of raping and abusing children. This level of documentation suggests a systematic approach to harnessing the spiritual power they believe comes from such heinous acts.

In the eyes of these Satanic sorcerers, every traumatized child is a sacrificial offering to the dark powers they serve. Each act of depravity is an occult ritual designed to give them dominion and influence in the spiritual realm.

The Call to Prayer Warfare As Christians, we cannot ignore the urgent spiritual implications of what the Epstein files have exposed. This is not just about bringing criminals to justice - it's about pulling back the veil on a global network of darkness that threatens us all.

The time has come to put on the full armor of God and enter into prayer warfare against the principalities and powers behind this evil. We must call upon the name of Jesus to break the strongholds of Satan and deliver the captives from this horrific abuse.

The stakes could not be higher. The Epstein files have laid bare the reality of spiritual darkness at the highest levels of power. Now is the hour for the church to rise up in faith and take our stand against the forces of evil.
