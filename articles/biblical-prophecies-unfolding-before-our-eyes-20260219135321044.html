A preview of the original investigative report from The Wise Wolf.

Apocalyptic Omens The signs of the times are all around us. Unfolding events match biblical prophecy with uncanny precision. As a worried parent, I can't ignore the ominous parallels staring us in the face.

Today, the technology for this "mark of the beast" is already in development. Brain chips and trackable e-wallets are becoming mainstream. The surveillance state is tightening its grip.

A megalomaniacal global leader is consolidating power, making bizarre end-times declarations, and mocking biblical morality. The Antichrist may be unmasking himself before our eyes.

What do the end times prophecies in the Bible mean?

The Bible's prophetic books like Daniel and Revelation describe a future time of global upheaval, oppressive governance, and supernatural conflict leading to the second coming of Jesus Christ and the establishment of His eternal kingdom.

Are the events we're seeing today signs of the end times?

Many of the specific prophetic signs foretold in Scripture are strikingly similar to the technological, geopolitical, and spiritual conditions emerging in our world. This suggests the end times timeline may be accelerating towards its climactic conclusion.
